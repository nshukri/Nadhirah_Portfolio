FIFA Mobile

One of the world's best soccer game on mobile

FIFA Mobile is an online PvP and PvE soccer game by Electronic Arts, where soccer fans have the opportunity to build their ultimate team with their favourite or best players in the world! FIFA Mobile has beenranked at the top in iOS App Store and Google Play Store.

In 2018, Electronic Arts collaborated with Tencent to publish FIFA Mobile China, a version of the game specifically for the Chinese market.
